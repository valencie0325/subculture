Where Broken Becomes Beautiful
At Kintsugi Studio, every broken bowl, every shattered plate, every chipped cup is invited to a quiet, sun-lit room in New Brunswick.
Here, under soft light and the gentle hum of lacquer, you will sit with strangers who become friends,
and together you will repair what was broken — not to make it “as good as new,” but to make it more honest, more alive, more you.
